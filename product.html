Description

The Scinomix SCI-PRINT VX2 is a small footprint multifunction vial handling instrument.

Its primary function is as a vial labeler. Other options include uncapping and capping (Screw top lids), filling and cherry picking. There is an optional built in 2D barcode scanner to ensure the vials are correctly placed in the racks (Matrix and Micronic). The SCI-PRINT VX2 is compatible with Matrix and Micronic 2D vials. It will also handle vials from 0.5 to 50ml in size. The SCI-PRINT VX2 will operate stand alone or can be integrated as part of a larger automated system.
